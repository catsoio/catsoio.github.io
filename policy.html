4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Delete your personal data

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@hemmafest.catso.io.
