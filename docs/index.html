8) Catso Robotics: Simulate → Train → Deploy (at Industrial Scale)

Catso builds and trains domain-specialized robots inside a high-fidelity digital environment—then deploys them into mines, plants, and logistics hubs with near-zero downtime. Think: autonomous inspection, materials handling, and maintenance for Nordic heavy industry (e.g., LKAB), ports, and energy facilities.

Why now

Robots no longer need years of risky on-site learning. Using world models + physics-grounded simulation, Catso generates millions of edge cases—variable lighting, dust, occlusions, slippery grades, blocked routes—before a real robot ever moves. Result: faster ramp, safer ops, and a measurable ROI on day one.

Value Proposition:60–90% faster training cycles, 30–50% fewer incidents, and 20–35% OPEX reduction in targeted workflows (pilot benchmarks; line-of-business dependent).

How it works
  1. Digitize the site: Lidar + CAD + telemetry → a live digital twin of drifts, shafts, conveyors, and loading bays.
  2. Train in sim: World-model-driven simulation produces physically plausible scenarios; policy learning runs at accelerated sim-time.
  3. Validate & certify: Safety envelopes, redundancy plans, and failover behaviors are stress-tested virtually.
  4. Deploy & adapt: Policies ship to robots; performance feeds back to the twin for continuous improvement.
